Chocolate Milk

Ice cream and soda. Two great things together make one phenomenal dessert.

Ingredients

  • Tall glass or mug, chilled
  • Three scoops ice cream, vanilla flavor recommended
  • Root beer, Barq's or A&W recommended

To make a root beer float:

  1. Scoop 2-3 scoops of ice cream into chilled mug
  2. Slowly add root beer to mug - try to limit the fizzing
  3. Let sit 2 minutes
  4. Sip slowly and enjoy (recommend straw and spoon)
