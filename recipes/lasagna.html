Lasagna

Flat pasta, tomato sause, a couple kinds of cheese, what else could you want?

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles, cooked
  • Tomato paste
  • Riccota cheese
  • Parsely
  • Mozzerrella cheese

To make a lasagna:

  1. Using a 9x11 inch dish, put a thin layer of tomato sauce on bottom of dish
  2. Cover tomato sause in dish with a layer of pasta
  3. Cover pasta with thin layer of tomato sauce, followed by a layer of ricotta cheese
  4. Repeat layering 2-3 times, adding in mozzerrella cheese as preferred
    5. Top with a layer of mozzerrella cheese and parsely
