Chocolate Milk

Rich, creamy, chocolatey milk. Mmmmm, mmmmmmm good.

Ingredients

  • Tall glass
  • Whole milk or almond milk (plain)
  • Chocolate syrup or powder

To make a glass of chocolate milk:

  1. Pour milk into a cold, tall glass (about 1 serving = 8 oz.)
  2. Add 2-3 tablespoons of chocolate syrup or powder
  3. Stir vigorously until all chocolate ingredients are dissolved and milk is chocolate color
  4. Add more chocolate flavoring as needed
  5. Sip slowly and enjoy
